In 2007, a young Indian cricket team, led by MS Dhoni, entered the first-ever T20 World Cup as underdogs. *They began with a thrilling bowl-out victory against Pakistan in the group stage.
*In the Super 8s, Yuvraj Singh's six sixes against England and strong bowling helped them reach the semi-finals.

*The final against Pakistan was a nail-biter. Joginder Sharma’s last-over heroics sealed a 5-run win.
India lifted the trophy, marking the start of a new era in cricket.
